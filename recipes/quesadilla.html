Quesadilla


Description

Nothing beats the good ol' chicken quesadilla. This is a classic dish that is sure to please your friends and family. Yum!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Marinade
  • Onion
  • Sauce
  • Tortillas
  • Mexican Cheese Blend

Steps

  • Marinade the chicken Breast and leave it in the fridge for at least 30 mins.
  • Put some oil on a frying pan and put in the chicken once it is hot.
  • Cut up the chicken in cubes once it is cooked. Cut the onion also in cubes.
  • Assemble the ingredients on half the torilla and put it on the stove.
  • Flip so both sides get cooked.
  • Serve!
