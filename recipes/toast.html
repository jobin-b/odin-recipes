Toast!


Description

Toast is Toast! Toasty bread.

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice
  • Topping (optional)

Steps

  • Throw your bread in the toaster.
  • It is now toast!
  • Pair it with some fruit or butter. Maybe even some eggs.
  • You can even use it to make French Toast.

Fun Fact: French Toast was not made by France!
